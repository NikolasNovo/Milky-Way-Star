1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the Milky Way Star mobile game, related websites, support communications, store listings, and services connected to the game. In this document, “we”, “us”, and “our” refer to Nikolas Novo / Quantum Box.

2. Information we collect or process

We aim to collect only the information needed to operate, improve, secure, and monetize the game. Depending on your device, region, platform, settings, and how you use the game, the following information may be collected or processed.

Gameplay data

  • Game progress, goals, scores, achievements, leaderboard scores, cloud save data, settings, preferences, and similar gameplay information.
  • Some gameplay data may be stored locally on your device.
  • Some gameplay data may be processed through Google Play Games Services, Apple Game Center, or similar platform services where available.

Device and technical data

  • Device model, operating system version, app version, language, country or region, time zone, screen information, and performance information.
  • Technical identifiers, such as app instance identifiers, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or platform identifiers, where permitted by your device settings and applicable law.
  • IP address and approximate location may be processed by third-party services for security, analytics, advertising, fraud prevention, and service delivery.

Advertising data

  • The game includes advertising.
  • Advertising partners may process data such as advertising identifier, device information, approximate location, ad views, ad interactions, app activity, and technical data.
  • Advertising may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your consent, device settings, age settings, platform rules, and regional requirements.

Analytics data

  • We use analytics, including Google Analytics for Firebase or similar services, to understand game usage and improve the game.
  • Analytics may include information such as session length, screens or features used, gameplay events, app opens, approximate region, device information, and app version.
  • Analytics helps us improve gameplay, performance, balance, usability, and stability.

Crash reporting and diagnostics

  • We use crash reporting and diagnostics if a crash, freeze, error, or technical issue occurs.
  • Crash reports may include crash logs, stack traces, device model, operating system version, app version, time of crash, technical diagnostics, and related performance data.
  • This information is used to find and fix bugs and improve stability.

In-app purchase data

  • The game may include in-app purchases.
  • Payments are processed by Google Play, Apple App Store, or another applicable app store provider.
  • We do not receive or store your full payment card details.
  • We may receive limited purchase-related information such as product ID, purchase status, transaction confirmation, refund status, or subscription status if subscriptions are offered.

Support data

  • If you contact us, we may process your email address, message content, attachments, and any information you choose to provide.
  • We use support data to respond to your request, investigate issues, and improve the game.

3. How we use information

  • To operate and maintain the game.
  • To save gameplay progress, scores, settings, achievements, leaderboards, and cloud saves.
  • To show ads and measure ad performance.
  • To process, verify, restore, and support in-app purchases.
  • To analyze game usage and improve gameplay, balance, performance, and usability.
  • To detect, investigate, and fix crashes, bugs, and technical issues.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, cheating, security incidents, and misuse.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations and platform requirements.

4. Advertising and tracking choices

The game shows ads through third-party advertising partners, such as Google AdMob or similar ad networks. These partners may use device identifiers, advertising identifiers, app activity, approximate location, and ad interaction data to provide, measure, limit, personalize, or secure ads.

Where required, we will ask for your consent before using personalized ads or tracking technologies. On Apple platforms, where applicable, the game may request permission through Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt before tracking you across apps and websites owned by other companies. If you deny permission or personalized advertising is not available, the game may show non-personalized ads.

You can manage advertising choices through your device settings, Google settings, Apple settings, consent prompts shown in the game, or other controls provided by advertising partners.

5. Analytics

We use analytics to understand how players interact with Milky Way Star and to improve the experience. Analytics may tell us, for example, whether a feature is used, whether a level or goal is too difficult, whether an update caused performance problems, or which app version is active.

Analytics is not intended to personally identify you. However, analytics providers may process technical identifiers and other data according to their own privacy policies and settings.

6. Crash reporting

If the game crashes or experiences a serious technical issue, crash reporting tools may collect diagnostic information. This helps us understand what went wrong and fix the issue. Crash reports may be processed automatically and may include device, app, and error details.

7. Google Play Games Services and Apple Game Center

The game may use platform gaming services, including Google Play Games Services and Apple Game Center where available. These services may provide features such as achievements, leaderboards, sign-in, player profiles, and cloud saves.

When you use these platform services, Google, Apple, or the relevant platform provider may process data connected to your profile, scores, achievements, saved games, and gameplay activity. Their processing is governed by their own privacy policies, account settings, and platform terms.

8. In-app purchases

The game may offer in-app purchases. Payments and billing are handled by Google Play, Apple App Store, or another applicable app store provider. We do not receive your full payment card information.

We may process limited purchase-related information to unlock content, verify purchases, restore purchases, provide support, prevent fraud, and comply with legal or tax obligations. You can manage purchases, refunds, and payment methods through your app store account settings.
